ABOUT PROJECT LET'S CONNECT

Design Indaba Mobile Application

Context

This is another project done during my second year at SFU for IAT235 Information Design. In a group of 4, we were asked to propose a design solution for the annual event Design Indaba Festival to enhance its participant’s overall experience, with an expectation to integrate in-depth primary research. My role in this project was to research the participant’s experience and design the Info & Comment page of the interface.

Challenge

For the exploring stage, I started by listing down all the problem framing that we could think of. As seen from the sticky-note board in Figure 1, we narrowed it down to the direction of “Idea sharing” and “Education & knowledge” after some initial research. However, I realize that some general secondary research online was not sufficient for us to pinpoint our user’s actual pain-point. Hence, the biggest challenge for me was to gather primary resource during COVID pandemic for a festival that is taking place in India.

Solution

Eventually, in order to get sufficient data, I reached out to over 100 people that has been to the festival on Instagram by following the tag #designindaba and browsing all the posts that has been linked with the event’s location. Although the respond rate is less that 10%, we managed to get enough data from our targeted demographics by letting them fill-up our survey questions and listed down the most helpful ones as shown in Figure 2.

This lead to our final design focus of supporting and engaging participants during their learning experience and encourage them to share their approaches with each other through a mobile application. The feature that I designed to address this is the Info & Comment page. The actual interface design was much smoother after accurately identifying our user’s needs as shown through my process in Figure 3 from sketches to high-fidelity mock-up.

Conclusion

In general, I am truly glad that have done this project early in my second year as it has taught me the importance of user research and actual data in providing a truly engaging user experience, which is also crucial for developing a functional interface.
